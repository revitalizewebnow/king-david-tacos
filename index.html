Who We Are

Hi, y'all. We're KDT.

We're a woman-owned, Brooklyn-founded breakfast taco company on a mission to make a high-quality, grab-and-go breakfast an easy choice for anyone.

We believe breakfast tacos are the perfect breakfast food — portable, satisfying, and endlessly delicious. Since opening our doors, we've been bringing a little bit of Texas breakfast magic to New York City.
